A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

A career scientist turned politician.

The President, Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the Prime Minister, Dr. Manmohan Singh and the Chairperson of UPA, Smt. Sonia Gandhi and other.

Here's a time line of Dr. Kalam's life:

  • 2014 : Doctor of Science award was given by Edinburgh University,UK.
  • 2013 : Von Braun Award was given by National Space Society.
  • 2012 : Doctor of Laws (Honoris Causa) was given by Simon Fraser University
  • 2011 : IEEE Honorary Membership was given by IEEE.
  • 2010 : Doctor of Engineering was given by University of Waterloo.
  • 2009 : Honorary Doctorate was given by Oakland University.
  • 2009 : Hoover Medal was given by ASME Foundation, USA.
  • 2009 : International von Kármán Wings Award was given by California Institute of Technology, USA.
  • 2008 : Doctor of Engineering (Honoris Causa) was given by Nanyang Technological University, Singapore
  • 2008 : Doctor of Science (Honoris Causa) was given by Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh.
  • 2007 : Honorary Doctorate of Science and Technology was given by Carnegie Mellon University.
  • 2007 : King Charles II Medal was given by Royal Society, UK.
  • 2007 : Honorary Doctorate of Science was given by University of Wolverhampton, UK.
  • 2000 : Ramanujan Award was given by Alwars Research Centre, Chennai.
  • 1998 : Veer Savarkar Award was given by Government of India
  • 1997 : Indira Gandhi Award for National Integration was given by Indian National Congress.
  • 1997 : Bharat Ratna was given by Government of India.
  • 1995 : Honorary Fellow was given by National Academy of Medical Sciences.
  • 1994 : Distinguished Fellow was given by Institute of Directors (India).
  • 1990 : Padma Vibhushan was given by Government of India.
  • 1981 : Padma Bhushan was given by Government of India.
